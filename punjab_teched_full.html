About Us

Punjab State Board of Technical Education & Industrial Training

📌 Established under the Punjab Act to regulate technical education and industrial training across the state.

Introduction

The Punjab State Board of Technical Education & Industrial Training (PSBTE&IT) is the apex body responsible for conducting examinations, granting affiliations to institutes, and regulating diploma-level technical education across the state of Punjab. The Board was established with a mandate to ensure quality technical education to the youth of Punjab, equipping them with industry-relevant skills and knowledge.

🎯

Vision

To become a world-class technical education board offering quality education and skill development to every student of Punjab.

🏆

Mission

Provide accessible, affordable, and quality technical education while maintaining the highest standards of examination and certification.

⚖️

Objectives

Regulate, supervise, and develop diploma-level technical education and industrial training institutes across Punjab.

🤝

Industry Connect

Bridge the gap between technical education and industry requirements through curriculum updates and internship programmes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PSBTE&IT? +
Punjab State Board of Technical Education & Industrial Training is the regulatory body for diploma-level technical education in Punjab. It affiliates institutes, conducts exams, and issues certificates.
What programmes does the Board offer? +
The Board offers 3-year Diploma programmes in Engineering and Non-Engineering fields including Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical, IT, and more.
How can I contact the Board? +
